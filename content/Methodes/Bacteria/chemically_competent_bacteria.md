## Procedure:
### Selecting a suitable Strain:

| Strain | Application | Features |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| E. coli DH5α | medium-yield plasmid DNA expression <br> (with blue/white screening) |  |
| E. coli TOP10 | high-yield plasmid DNA expression <br> (with blue/white screening) |  |
| E. coli XL1blue | plasmid DNA expression <br> with blue/white screening |  |
| E. coli BL21(DE3) | protein expression | lacUV5 promoter <br> for IPTG induced expression of t-RNA polymerase |
| E. coli BL21(DE3) pLysS | (toxic) protein expression | T7 Inhibitor |
| E. coli Rosetta(DE3) | (eucaryotic) protein expression | lacUV5 promoter <br> for IPTG induced expression of t-RNA polymerase |
| E. coli C41(DE3) | toxic protein expression | lacUV5 promoter <br> for IPTG induced expression of t-RNA polymerase |
| E. coli C43(DE3) | toxic membrane protein expression | lacUV5 promoter <br> for IPTG induced expression of t-RNA polymerase |

### Preparations:
1. <input type="checkbox"> Prepare one LB-agar Plate without any antibiotics.
2. <input type="checkbox"> Prepare 110 mL of LB medium.
### Plating Bacteria:
1. <input type="checkbox"> Choose a desired strain of Escherichia coli cells.
2. <input type="checkbox"> Plate a one shot directly on a LB-agar Plate.
3. <input type="checkbox"> Let the bacteria grow over night @ 37 °C.
### Pre-culture:
1. <input type="checkbox"> Transfer 1 mL of the 110 mL LB medium into a photometer cuvette for the zero value.
2. <input type="checkbox"> Transfer 5 mL of the 109 mL LB medium into a sterile 15 mL falcon tube.
3. <input type="checkbox"> Pick a suitable clone with pipette tip.
4. <input type="checkbox"> Drop pipette tip into the 5 mL of LB medium.
5. <input type="checkbox"> Incubate pre-culture @ 37 °C and shaking @ 250 rpm for approximately 2–4 h until an OD<sub>600</sub> ~ 0.4–0.8 (best 0.6) is reached or incubate @ 30 °C and shaking @ 250 rpm overnight.

### Main-culture:
1. <input type="checkbox"> Transfer 1 mL of the 104 mL LB medium into a photometer cuvette for the zero value.
2. <input type="checkbox"> Transfer 100 mL LB-medium into a sterile 200 mL Erlenmeyer flask.
3. <input type="checkbox"> Inoculate the 100 mL LB-medium with the pre-culture.
4. <input type="checkbox"> Incubate batch @ 37 °C and shaking @ 250 rpm for approximately 2–4 h until an OD<sub>600</sub> of 0.4 to 0.8 (best 0.6) is reached.

### Preparations:
1. <input type="checkbox"> Prepare 50 mL of Inoue transformation buffer from your 10x stock or by supplementing 50mL of PIPES (or alternatively MOPES) buffer 10 mM (pH 6.8 with acetic acid) with MnCl<sub>2</sub> 55 mM, CaCl<sub>2</sub> 15 mM and KCl 250 mM and cool on ice until further use.
2. <input type="checkbox"> Prepare 10 mL of Inoue storage buffer by supplementing 8 mL of Inoue transformation buffer with 750 µL of DMSO (7.5 %) and 1250 µL of Glycerol 85% (10.625 %) and cool on ice until further use.
3. <input type="checkbox"> Pre-cool 80 x sterile 1.5 mL reaction tubes.
4. <input type="checkbox"> Pre-cool a centrifuge to 4 °C for harvesting @ 3000–6000 g. 
### Cell harvesting:
1. <input type="checkbox"> Transfer the 100 mL of culture into 2 x sterile 50 mL falcon tubes.
2. <input type="checkbox"> Harvest cells by centrifugation @ 3000–6000 g and @ 4 °C for 10 minutes.
{{< timer time="0:10:00" >}}

3. <input type="checkbox"> Discard the medium and let pellet dry overhead on paper towels for 2 minutes.
{{< timer time="0:02:00" >}}

### Generating Competency:
1. <input type="checkbox"> Resuspend the pellet in 16 mL of ice-cold Inoue transformation buffer each by rotating end-over-end.
2. <input type="checkbox"> Harvest cells by centrifugation @ 3000–6000 g and @ 4 °C for 10 minutes.
{{< timer time="0:10:00" >}}

3. <input type="checkbox"> Discard the medium and let pellet dry overhead on paper towels for 2 minutes.
{{< timer time="0:02:00" >}}

### Storage:
1. <input type="checkbox"> Resuspend the pellet in 4 mL of ice-cold Inoue storage buffer each by rotating end-over-end for 10 minutes.
{{< timer time="0:10:00" >}}

2. <input type="checkbox"> Prepare some liquid nitrogen.
3. <input type="checkbox"> Aliquot the cells in 100 µL fractions in the pre-cooled sterile 1.5 mL reaction tubes.
4. <input type="checkbox"> Immediately freeze aliquots in liquid nitrogen.
5. <input type="checkbox"> Store the one shots at –60 °C.
{{< infobox type="info" title="" >}}
⚠️ Cells from here on are extremely sensitive to temperature increases.
