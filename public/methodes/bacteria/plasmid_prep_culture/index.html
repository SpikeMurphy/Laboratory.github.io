Procedure:

Preparation:

  1. Transform a suitable strain of bacteria and plate transformed bacteria on a LB-agar Plate containing appropriate selection markers (Antibiotic, X-Gal).
  2. Prepare 6 mL LB-medium per clone.

Culture:
